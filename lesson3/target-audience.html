Target Audience

The target audience of this site are parents and students who are looking for information on the storm activity in the Philippines. They are usually looking for a trusted source when it comes to class and work suspensions. They are usually ages 13-50. They use their mobile phones, with varying sizes, to access the website.

Persona

Name: Chris

Occupation: Salesman

Age: 43 years old

General Information: A husband and a father of three. Owns a car, but sometimes commute to work when it is more convenient.

Goals:Needs to know class cancellations that can happen in different cities due to storm activity before his kids leave for school. Uses his phone to access the information. Needs to know which route to take in order to know the safest way to get to work.

Name: Faith

Occupation: Student

Age: 14 years old

General Information: A high school student who goes to school early in the morning.

Goals:Needs to know up to date and reliable class cancellations in the city she lives in. Uses her phone to access the information easier than watching TV to get such information.

Scenarios

  • Will classes be cancelled today because of the weather?
  • Is this information reliable and up to date?
  • What route should I take to get to work?
  • Is it better to commute or to use my car
  • What do I need to wear to dress for the weather?
