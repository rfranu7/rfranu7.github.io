By Randeep Ranu

While there is certainly a time and a place for kite-flying with the crowds at Gas Works Park, or spreading out s'mores at a rowdy Golden Gardens, sometimes some of us just want a peaceful picnic away from it all. So, we've rounded up five more secret spots around Seattle where squealing busses and shouting crowds is substituted with light breezes and an actual ambiance of chirping birds.
