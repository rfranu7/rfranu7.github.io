About Us

Salmon River Adventures river rafting tour company is located in Riggins, Idaho and offers world-class, whitewater rafting experiences on the Salmon River in the middle of an Idaho wilderness. The owner-operated company offers various trips for groups and individuals using state-of-the-art equipment and complies with all U.S. Forest Service regulations.
